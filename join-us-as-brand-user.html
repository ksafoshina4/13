A Look Into Our Innovative Tools.
AI-Powered Influencer Search Tool

We have developed an innovative & easy-to-use search tool that allows you to search for profiles fittings your needs amongst millions of Instagram profiles. The tool filters profiles according to gender, location, age, followers & following numbers, engagement rate, age, and keywords. The more you search - the more accurate results our tool will give you. Find your tribe, ambassadors, or brands to collaborate with. Quick and easy.

Create Your Brand Profile

By joining our Brand Category you get a wonderful opportunity to register your brand, create & customize your brand profile, and become a member of our amazing ecosystem of international women. You will be in the middle of thousands of women who can buy your products.
Create your profile, highlight your products and link them to your e-commerce store, add links to other social media platforms of your brand, follow our users & brands, and get followed back. Having a profile at Fashion Potluck gives you yet another wonderful channel and a high-ranked link to your brand.

Share Your Branded Content

Now that you have your profile, you can fill it in with a variety of posts - sharing your commercial videos, images, and text. Have a launch party? Share it with our community. Just started a seasonal sale? Share it with our tribe too.
Share your most precious moments with our community and get their love and engagement back. Now, you've got a bunch of posts, that increase your brand awareness and boost your SEO.

Boost Your Posts Through Multiple Channels

Each time you share a post, you can increase the reach it gets by using our Instant Influencer Marketing tool. All you need to do is to choose mediums and the amount of posts per each and to fill in your CTA, URL, #tags, slogan, and regions.
Five minutes of your time and your post will increase its reach by a number of times. Our team will take care of all the details of the boost, making sure everything is done in accordance with your brand values. You will get a full report upon completion. Most importantly - the reach is guaranteed!

Highlight Your Bestsellers on Our Homepage

A cherry on top of our cake is - the FP Wishlist. The FP Wishlist is a moving bar highlighting the bestselling products of our brand users. It appears on the homepage and in the category pages. This means, your products have the potential to be viewed by over 60.000 women monthly.
You selected a product you want to show in our wishlist and, of course, it will lead to your e-commerce product page. Enjoy our platform, increase your brand awareness and ROI. Welcome to Fashion Potluck!
