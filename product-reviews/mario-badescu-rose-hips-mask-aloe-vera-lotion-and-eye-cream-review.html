Mario Badescu: Rose Hips Mask, Aloe Vera Lotion and Eye Cream Review

Product details: Mario Badescu Rose Hips Mask, Mario Badescu Aloe Vera Lotion, Mario Badescu Eye Cream

8 448 views
  • by Una Omeragic 26 followers
FOLLOW UNA

    Have you tried Mario Badescu Skincare Products? Here is my full review!

    Summer is over and my tan is fading quicker than I have planned! However, this year, I have finally decided to stop complaining about the summer-fall transition and instead use this period to try out different cosmetics brand for this season. 

    As I am on the journey of adopting beauty products which have a natural base and fewer chemicals, I am curious to try brands which I believe might fit this category. (P.S. if you have some recommendations about natural beauty products you loved, it would be great if you share it in the comments ;))

    About Mario Badescu

    Although I am familiar with Mario Badescu, I have never actually tried any of their products and it seems like I rarely see any reviews about this cosmetics brand. I am very curious about brands I don’t encounter every day, so I have decided it is time to check this brand out ;)

    For this review, I have bought several products including a face mask, lotion, and eye cream. Curious to know my impressions? Keep on reading ;)

    Mario Badescu Rose Hips Mask - $20.00

    Anyone who knows me, knows that I absolutely love anything which smells like roses, so when browsing through the Mario Badescu Face Masks, this indeed was my first choice, and a good one, I must say!

    After summer, I always try to nourish my skin with some extra oil as the sun dries it out a bit. And since this Rose Hips Mask is a combination of rose extract and oil, it helped me restore a healthy glow. 

    Do you also notice some discoloration spots after tanning? Well, you’re not the only one! Since this happens to me quite often, I always try to apply products which contain antioxidant Vitamin C that helps brighten my skin. After using the Rose Hips Mask, it helped minimize several discoloration spots and soften fine lines leaving my skin soft and fresh. 

    How to use it: When applying face masks, I always prefer the ones which feel light on my skin, and the Mario Badescu does the job. It is very simple to use: wash your face with clean water, dry it, and apply a thin layer to your skin. Leave it for around 20 minutes and rinse well with warm water. Also, try to avoid the eye area to prevent any irritation.  

    The mask comes in a nicely designed white bottle and I believe it will last up to 3 months if I use it once a week, which I find super economical! 

    You can order the mask at Mario Badescu for $20.00.

    Mario Badescu Aloe Vera Lotion - $15.00


    Aloe Vera always sounds like a good idea! I have decided to try this Aloe Vera Lotion to add another refreshing product to my beauty bag, but to be honest, this product surprised me quite a bit. I expected it to be a lotion, but it is a cleansing product! 

    When I tried it, it cleansed my skin and removed all dirt and makeup, leaving it refreshed. However, I don’t feel as if it improved my skin complexion that much besides removing all impurities. As Aloe Vera contains a rich source of antioxidants and vitamins that help protect your skin, it can also help reduce the frequency and appearance of acne. Because of this feature, I believe this product is more suitable for women with oily skin who suffer from different skin conditions.

    How to use it: Apply the lotion using a pad after cleansing your skin. Use it twice a day, after your morning and evening routine. 

    You can order the lotion for $15.00 at Mario Badescu here.

    If you’re looking for a product that will help you spot acne and act fast on it, then I actually recommend checking another product by Mario Badescu, the Drying Lotion.


    Containing salicylic acid and calamine, it will help dry out blemishes, prevent acne spread and balance skin oil. To make the product as efficient as possible, you should apply it overnight and rinse off in the morning. You can find an in-depth review of the lotion here and order this popular product for only $17.00 on their site.

    Mario Badescu Eye Cream – $18.00


    For this season I have decided to start using eye creams as I just can’t get enough sleep during the week which naturally leads to annoying dark circles under my eyes. 

    Although Mario Badescu Eye Cream is one of the first eye creams I have ever used, I must admit I enjoyed its lightweight formula. After two weeks of use, I have already started noticing a difference and my under eyes feel smoother and lighter. 

    As well, this eye cream contains hyaluronic acid which helps retain skin moisture and fight aging effects. Besides moisturizing effects, I enjoy using this eye cream as I find it ideal for normal/dry skin such as mine. Next to hyaluronic acid, the cream contains Safflower Seed Oil which is high in essential fatty acids and keeps my skin nourished and soft. 

    How to use it: You can use this eye cream every day.  I usually apply a thin layer under my makeup using my finger. You can order the Mario Badescu eye cream for $18.00 on their site.


    My overall score for Mario Badescu products is a solid 7/10! The brand’s philosophy is providing simple, gentle and effective skincare, and I must admit they’re doing a great job so far! Do you have any experience using their products? Let me know in the comments below!

    Una
