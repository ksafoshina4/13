Assumptions About Me

5 84 views
  • by Srishti Singh 9 followers
FOLLOW SRISHTISINGH

          It's been long overdue and I wanted to do assumptions about me blogpost. Thank you for asking me questions, makes me understand you guys better and makes our bond stronger. Well, it was super fun to read all the assumptions you guys had about me. 

          It really had me thinking about how crazy social media can be, and how so much of what you see is a highlight reel… there’s so much more that goes on behind the scenes that no one ever sees! 
            
          So let's get started  
            
          HOW MUCH DO YOU SHOP 

          Well, this is a bit funny, I call myself a dedicated shopper. Yes, I shop a lot but always make sure I get a better price of the item that I like. Sometimes that's why I wait for sales. As there is no point of paying the double price for a piece when you can get it in almost half price after some time. Additionally, I love stores and that is also one of the reasons I just wander there. That's why you might see me every Friday in the stores. Makes me happy, makes me forget everything problem that exists.
            
            
          YOU DON’T BUY CHEAP CLOTHES / ALL YOUR CLOTHES ARE BEAUTIFUL  

          Like I said, I shop mostly in the sale. Getting something good at a good price is always exciting. But I always look for something a bit out of the box, cute and something that's me.  
            
          YOU’RE BOUGIE AF 

           Very funny, but NO. I just appreciate fine things in life and like to live a certain way. I never forget the road which took me all the way here. I respect everything that has happened, good or bad, it all was a very good learning experience for me.


          To read more. Please head to: https://www.confessionofmusings.com/2020/01/assumptions-about-me.html
