FAVORITE 3.14JACK

    Hey, the Fashion Potluck community <3

    We are happy to be a part of it and would like to start our journey here with an introduction as to who we are and what we do. You will notice, that our communication through other social media platforms is minimal, therefore here we would like to share our processes and thoughts in more detail.

    We are 3.14jack.

    It is read as [pi-ˈdʒæk], and is inspired by the word [пиджак], which means a jacket in Russian. 3.14 consequently, represents a number Pi.  

    We are a sustainable fashion brand, creating ethically made & reconstructed suits, jackets, and shirts. Our current values are minimalism (in designs & communication), transparency, sustainability (zero waste ⚬ upcycling ⚬ high-quality ⚬ fair labor).

    In the following articles, we will dive deep into each one of these. For now, though, let us introduce you to one of our suits — SN11.

    SN11 UPCYCLED SUIT

    SN11 is a re-constructed black pinstripe suit with a single-breasted jacket & frilled mini-shorts. High-quality, charming, and unique.

    — Fitted jacket

    — Three buttons

    — Two side pockets

    — Shortened back of the jacket, allowing for the visibility of the shorts

    — Frilled mini-shorts

    — Lining: Viscose 100%

    — Shell: Wool 100%
