Horoscope for October 2019. Mischief, Danger, or Love. Let's find out!

6 204 views
  • by Alexandra Wassermann 6 followers
FOLLOW ALEXANDRAWASS

    Hi ladies, my name is Alexandra and I'm working as an astrologist, providing personal consultations and horoscopes. Here, I would like to share with you the monthly horoscopes. Starting today, you can find my posts here. Also, don't forget to follow me on my IG for other updates: @astrologwass.

    ARIES

    21.03 – 20.04

    Women, who were born between the 23rd and 26th of March have to be prepared for solving complex problems, overcoming obstacles, and hard work. The slogan for the coming month should be "Patience and labor will grind everything”. Pay special attention to your health. For those born between the 8th and 10th of April, it is important to restrain your increased internal aggression and the desire to solve all problems by rapid force methods.

    TAURUS

    20.04 – 21.05

    In October, Taurus will have a favorable period for improving existing relationships in marriage and business, as well as for starting new ones. Those born on the 20th – 22nd of April will have a period of drastic changes and instability in October. There’s a possibility that some external circumstances will occur, which will require a drastic change in life, the desire for a revolution in your life. Let’s remember, the revolutions organized by Taurus are capable of destroying not only their life but the whole world. Do not take any rash steps. Those born between the 13th and 21st of May might suddenly have an excessive self-conceit and a tendency to excessive, uncontrollable actions in various areas of life. Avoid overly optimistic assessments of the things you’re doing. Remember that excessive pomp and self-centeredness don’t suit you.

    GEMINI

    22.05 – 20.06

    October will be a particularly favorable period for creating a more pleasant environment in your workplace or improving work processes. Take care of your health and physical work, because at the moment this is what will bring great returns. Those born between the 2nd and 6th of June will find it especially difficult to perceive reality as it is. Be wary of an increased propensity for self-deception, distraction, lack of discipline and isolation from life realities. Try not to abuse alcohol.

    CANCER

    21.06 – 22.07

    October is favorable for love, romantic meetings, hobbies or parenting. In October, those born on the 24th – 27th of June will need to work hard to overcome a bunch of problems that have accumulated. Pay special attention to your health. In the life of Cancers born on the 9th – 12th of July, global and powerful transformational processes will continue that will lead to a new stage in life.

    LEO

    23.07 – 22.08

    In October, pay attention to and take care of your family questions and real estate. Make your home more comfortable and your family happier. For Leos, born on the 23rd – 25th of July, October will bring a period of unexpected and drastic changes. Although Leo is the queen of animals, sometimes she is not able to influence the circumstances that dramatically and radically change her life. Those born between the 15th and 22nd of August may show excessive self-confidence and a tendency to narcissism. Leos have enough of those from their birth, therefore, try to stay down to earth.

    VIRGO

    23.08 – 22.09

    October will be favorable for traveling on short distances, communicating with neighbors and relatives, and self-education. Those born from the 6th to 10th of September within a month may have a desire to get away from the harsh realities of life. There’s a likelihood of you being prone to self-deception, distraction, lack of discipline, and isolation from life. Try not to abuse alcohol, rather get into something creative. There may appear situations when you will have to take care of young children or elderly relatives.

    LIBRA

    23.09 – 22.10

    October is favorable for generating additional income, acquiring personal property. If you have dreamt of acquiring something important for a long time, then you can safely do it this month. Those born between the 24th and 28th of September need to work hard to overcome many problems that have accumulated over a long time. Pay special attention to your health. In the life of those born between the 10th and 13th of October, global changes will continue to happen. You might only understand the meaning of these changes after years. Do not let rage and aggression to overflow you, remember that Libra is one of the most peaceful signs of the zodiac.

    SCORPIO

    23.10 – 21.11

    In October, avoid conflicts in the family, do not torment them with your painful Scorpio bites, try to find other uses for them. If excessive energy overwhelms you and provokes a conflict, try using this energy for repairing or cleaning your house. Scorpions born on the 24th -26th of October may experience quick and unexpected changes in life, as well as the tendency to commit unusual actions. It’s better to prepare for these changes in advance. Those born between the 16th and 21st of November will have an important period of professional development and growth. Feel free to conduct financial operations, during this period you can get promoted, achieve a higher social or professional position.

    SAGITTARIUS

    22.11 – 21.12

    October is favorable for diving deep into your subconscious, for solitude in order to search for spiritual truth. For those born on the 4th – 8th of December, this month will become a period of moving away from the complexities of real-life into the world of illusions. It is also likely to run into deceit. Try not to abuse alcohol. Striving into the future, do not get lost along the way in your dreams, they are quite limitless already.

    CAPRICORN

    22.12 – 19.01

    For Capricorns, October is favorable for any communication with friends and like-minded people. It is a good time to launch an online project or participate in informal associations. Those born between the 22nd and 26th of December will need to work hard to overcome many problems at home and at work. But overcoming obstacles and hard work is a common thing for Capricorns, so this period will not become too critical for you. Pay special attention to your health. Long-term powerful transformational processes will continue in the lives of those born on the 8th – 11th of January 8-11, the result of which will appear only after a few years. Relieve your overwhelming energy regularly through hard work.

    AQUARIUS

    20.01 – 18.02

    In October there will be a favorable period for your career. This month you can confidently expand your professional horizons and hope for public recognition of your merits. Those with the sign Aquarius, born on the 21st – 23rd of January, will face a period of dramatic unexpected changes and extraordinary actions.

    PISCES

    19.02 – 20.03

    For Pisces, October will be a favorable month for travels, philosophical research, education, communication with representatives of other cultures. Those born on the 2nd – 5th of March are likely to be manipulated this month. There is also a danger of going into the world of illusions. Solar Pisces from birth have a tendency to fly far from reality, however, during October they should try to be as down to earth and realistic as possible.


    I hope you enjoyed this horoscope. And, see you again next month!♥
