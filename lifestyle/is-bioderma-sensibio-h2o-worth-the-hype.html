Is Bioderma Sensibio H2O Worth The Hype?

5 1 32 views
  • by Shreemayee Chattopadhyay 10 followers
FOLLOW SHREEMAYEESDIARY

    Bioderma’s Sensibio H2O has steered it’s way as one of the most popular and easily available micellar water worldwide. But while it’s emerging at the forefront of the micellar water trend, Is Bioderma’s Sensibio H2O worth the hype?

     

    In today’s world where we are bombarded with information about the best skincare routines and products to use for optimal face cleansing, Bioderma’s micellar water stands out of the crowd.

     


    What is Sensibio H2O?

     

    Not only does it remove stubborn makeup from your face and eyes, while cleansing your skin of impurities and other irritants, it also leaves your skin feeling hydrated and moisturized. All of this, without any use of running water whatsoever. Bioderma micellar water is the closest thing to genie in a bottle. This makes Sensibio H2O a must have for all those ladies travelling back from work or mothers hitting the gym for those quick workouts after finishing off their chores. 

     

     

     

    How does it work?

    Sensibio H2O is dermatologically compatible with the skin. It comprises tiny molecules that are micelles. Now we could tell you micelle are tiny droplets of oil suspended in soft water, which also look like water but have different texture and feel to it. But in simpler terms, micelles are oil-like molecules that act like magnets to your skin and attract makeup, dirt and other impurities and draw them out without harming your skin. 

     

     

    Who can use it?

    Although Sensibio H2O is suitable for all skin types, we recommend it for people with dry and sensitive skin because it’s soothing and maintains the pH level of your skin. It is unfragranced and mild, so you can even use it on your eyes without worrying about any kind of irritation or allergic reaction. 

     

     

    How can I use it?

     

    • As a makeup remover:

    If you are one of those professional working females or even a gym rat mother of two, we know you still like that eyeliner on fleek. Using a normal makeup remover requires pretty harsh rubbing around the eye area to get it completely cleaned, which could be rough on your skin. Sensibio H2O is a gentle make up remover. It even removes waterproof makeup with ease, leaving your skin clean and smooth with just a few wipes.

     

    • For dry skin:
      People who have dry skin that is prone to cracks and acne can use it as their go-to cleanser. Sensibio H2O soothes and clears up irritants laying dormant on your skin, letting it breathe and letting you feel instantly fresh.

     

    • For Oily skin:

    There’s nothing more haunting than that greasy glow on our forehead and cheeks, resting on our face like it belongs there. Bioderma’s micellar water cleans out excess oil off of your skin, while maintaining pH balance so you get rid of that halo looking structure on your cheeks.

     

     

    Where can you get it?

    You can order Sensibio H2O from our friends at Amazon and Nykaa and let it be a perfect addition to your list of cleansers and makeup remover needs!
