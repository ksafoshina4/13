NSALE Top Picks!

Product details:

3 57 views
  • by Alyvia Valkanos 29 followers
FOLLOW LIFEWITHLYV

    The day is here! The NSALE is finally live baby! I have a separate post all about tips and tricks for shopping this years sale! You can reference back for the different access dates for each Nordy Club tier there! But in this post I am rounding up all my favorite pieces from each category! And let me tell ya honey, there are some AWESOME pieces included in this years sale that you won't want to miss! So grab your Nordstrom Card and let's get down to business...



    NSALE Home Favorites

    SO many fun home finds included in this years NSALE! I typically take advantage of the great prices and stock up on fun holiday gifts for friends and family! Never too early to start thinking about Christmas if you ask me!


    Barefoot Dreams

    Barefoot Dreams items are always some of the first to go! They are a tad bit on the pricey side, and they rarely ever go on sale... So if you've ever wondered what all the hype was about, now is the time to take the leap and buy yourself one! They make super awesome gifts also! I gifted my mom and my MIL blankets last year for Christmas and they both LOVED them!

     

    NSALE Women's Fashion Favorites

    Tops

    Free People Henley Top | White T-shirt (comes in 7 other colors) | Black Lace Trim Satin Bodysuit | Animal Print Cozy V-Neck Tunic | Black Cowl Neck Satin Camisole | Topshop Long Sleeve Square Neck Bodysuit 


    Sweaters

    Madewell Waffle Stitch Colorblock Sweater | BDG Urban Outfitters Shaker Stitch Cardigan | Cowl Neck Sweater | Blush Stripe Crewneck Sweater | Barefoot Dreams Cozy Chic Cardigan | Olive Green Open Stitch Cardigan


    Coats/Outerwear 

    I am that girl who feels the need to buy new coats every year... When it comes to jackets, I end up just tossing them in the back seat or flinging them over the nearest chair whenever I get somewhere. Which means they never survive long. Lol. But that's just one more reason I love the NSALE! I always end up finding super cute and really fun new pieces for those impending colder Michigan days!

    I also included my FAVORITE leather biker jacket that has actually survived my closet for the last couple seasons! Such a staple piece!


    Bottoms 

    If you haven't caught on by now, this sale is MY sale... I try to refrain from buying new bottoms or any new articles of clothing for fall until this sale. It's the best time to load up on jeans or fun skirts for the upcoming season!


    Shoes

    Probably one of the biggest parts of the NSALE as well as my favorite category to shop every year... Perfect time to snag new arrivals from the bigger named brands! Can't lie, I went overboard on boots last year!


    Accessories

    Gimme ALL the accessories! Another one of my favorite parts of the NSALE every year! I am a sunglasses and scarf junkie, but they always have so many other cute accessories to compliment those fall outfits as well!


    Handbags

    JUST THROW IT IN MY TOTE BAG! If you haven't seen 'For A Good Time, Call' you totally just missed that reference. Lol. But coming from the bag lady herself over here, this sale always has some super cute and super fun options when it comes to handbags!


    Designer

    This is hands down the time to jump on the designer band wagon if you haven't already! Brands that RARELY go on sale are offering huge discounts. I'm talking discounts that will have you wanting to buy two of everything!

     

    NSALE Beauty Favorites

     

    NSALE Kids/Baby Favorites

    Kids

    This was always one of my go-to sales for back to school shopping... Obviously who knows if the kiddos will even being going back to school this fall, but still a great time to pick up some cute pieces for your minis!



    Baby

    Expecting a baby, or have a friend who is expecting? This is the perfect time to stock up on big ticket items for yourself or gifts for all the soon to be mamas in your life! This is definitely a great time to snag a carseat or stroller!


    I am a creature of habit and every morning I scan through Nordstrom.com just to be sure I can help find the best pieces! With that being said, I will be updating this page frequently! So be sure to bookmark this post and SUBSCRIBE to my blog for more sale updates, restock alerts and maybe even some more awesome finds!
