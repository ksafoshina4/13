VISIT LONDON IN THREE DAYS

                                                       VISIT LONDON IN THREE DAYS  


       Hello girlssss,I’m a London Lover Girl, I visited London City three time.

    My first time in London was on November 2012,my second time on December 2018 and the third time two months ago,on Jenuary 2020.  

    The best time to visit London is from November to December because the city is very decked for Christmas time with beautiful light .    


    OOK GILRSSS,WE START  WITH TRAVEL GUIDE TO VISIT IN 3 DAYYS LONDON CITY:


    FIRST DAY:   

     


    IN THE MORNING:   

    Take a bus or Metro and get until Westminter stop,when you arrive  can you visit :

    -BIG BEN AND PARLAMENT OF LONDON   

    - LONDON EYE (you can buy the tickets online before the trip,there are often long lines at the checkouts) , I raccomend taking a ride on the London Eye because you can see the best view of London especially at the sunset.The ride takes about 30 minutes.This is the best experience of my life.

    -WESTMISNTER ABBEY   

    - TAMIGI RIVER  can you walking on the street up the Tamigi river, l think that this is the best zone of London because the atmosfere is wonderful thanks to the buskers like singers,dancers and jugglers.

    IN THE EVENING:   

    After lunch you can go to visit:

    - TOWER BRIDGE: this is the most famous bridge in London, starting to see it from afar as you get closer is a feeling that gives a thousand emotions.

    One time you arrive near at the bridge    YOU CAN TAKE MORE PHOTOS 

     


    after taking the photos continue towards the bridge and cross it, its two towers are indescribable from up close, the buses that cross it and the famous London taxis make everything magical.   

    -CONVENT GARDEN: after visiting the bridge, you can go to Cvent Garden, a beautiful area of London where with its many pubs of all ethnicities you can spend the evening and enjoy a good dinner before returning to the hotel.   


    SECOND DAY

    IN THE MORNING:   

    To better start your second day in London, I recommend you choose one of the El & n coffe stores closest to your accommodation area to have a good and rich breakfast, from there we start our day.   



    The El&N COFFE is the coffee bar very famous to London,is very beautiful,all the wall are color pink and the glasses,the fork,the cake,the biscuits are pink.   


    HYDE PARK - GREEN PARK: take the metro and get to Hyde park, from there you can start  a relaxing walk to the most famous parks of Lodra, the parks stretch for kilometers and kilometers, inside them you can find kiosks and many types of animals , among the most famous SQUIRRELS are really sweet, you can also buy special feed for these animals it will be a beautiful emotion when you stretch your hands and slowly get closer.   

    BUCKINGAM PALACE: once you have walked through the parks and taken some photos,you will finally find yourself in front of the queen's palace, majestic, immense and unique. If you arrive at the 11.30 a.m. you can see the famous CHANGE OF THE GUARD .   


    ST.JAMES PARK: Once the visit to the Queen's palace is finished, to return by metro, go through St. James Park, one of the most well-kept and beautiful royal parks in London, you can also take the opportunity to eat something lying on the lawn, if it is sunny, everything is even more magical.   


    IN THE EVENING:   


    PICADILLY CIRCUS: After having lunch take the tube or bus that will take you to the Picadilly Circus station from here you can start your crazy afternoon of shopping, take the usual photos and then continue through the most famous fashion streets of London, they are all here.   

                                        This the more famous street to shopping:


    ⦁    OXFORD STREET

    ⦁    REGENT STREET

    ⦁    COVENTRY STREET

    ⦁    THE HAYMARKET


    Do not miss a ride from HARRODS the large luxury shopping center in London in:   


    ⦁    BROMPTON ROAD



    DINNER TIMEEEE:   

    Do you like a pizza? I’m a pizza lover and I had  Pizza in every place I visited

    In London there are more PIZZERIE NAPOLITAN , I reccomend PIZZERIA FRATELLI LA BUFALA , the pizza is very good.            

            


                                                                                    ADDRESS PIZZERIA:   

                                                                                        Fratelli La Bufala   

                                                      Location: 40,Shaftesbury Avenue | Piccadilly, Londra W1D 7EY   


    THIRD DAY              

    This is the last day in the best European City,are you ready?


    N THE MORNING:

    NOTTING HILL: Once you get off at the NOTTING HILL tube or bus stop, get lost in the most elegant district of London, its colorful houses, the tree-lined streets will make you feel like a fairy tale.   


    PORTOBELLO ROAD: Once you have visited all Notting Hill and taken many photos, head towards Portobello Road.

    This is one of the most colorful and famous markets in London. There are thousands of people who buy from the famous stalls.   



    This is your last day in London, I list some famous square and some free museum, you can choose what to visit:  

    TRAFALGAR SQUARE   

    LEICESTER SQUARE IN SOHO is a beautiful and famous zone of London,walking you can visit CHINATOWN ii is AMAZING   

    BRITISH MUSEUM - Free   

    DESIGN MUSEUM - Free   

    MUSEUM OF LONDON - Free   

    NATURAL HISTORY MUSEUM - Free   

    SCIENCE MUSEUM - Free   

    VICTORIA AND ALBERT MUSEUM - Free --> This to girl loves STYLEEE
