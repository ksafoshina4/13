What To Watch On Disney+ - My TV Show & Movie Recommendations

6 28 views
  • by Ella Beecroft 9 followers
FOLLOW LIFESTYLESEASON

    Hello! Hope you're okay! Today's blog post is all about what to watch on Disney+! Since the release of star, there is even more movies and TV shows to watch on Disney+. This post is a list of my favourite TV shows and movies on Disney+ that you should watch! I hope you like this post and find something new to watch (or just one of your favourites) Let me know if you want to see a part two in the future and make sure to comment some of your Disney+ recommendations!

    Disney/Pixar

    Tangled

    I watched this movie a few days ago and I love it! The songs are really good and the characters are great! I’ve watched it so many times now (I actually watched it in the cinemas when it came out over 10 years ago now) and it never gets boring. I love how different it is from other disney princess movies!

    Beauty And The Beast

    I love both the live action and the animated film! If I had to pick a favourite, I would have to say the live action one because I LOVE the actors and I prefer the singing but I would still recommend watching both of the movies to see which one you prefer. The songs are amazing and again I could watch this so many times without getting bored of it!


    Up

    This is an incredible film! There is a really good balance between emotional moments and comedic parts which I think makes this film really good. I love the characters so much and the storyline is great! I would highly recommend this film to anyone because I feel like it is for a variety of ages.


    Finding Nemo/Finding Dory

    Finding Nemo is such a classic film that I love watching! Both of the films are great because they are quite different from each other but there are some connections at times. The characters are so good and I really like how both of the films have some sort of adventure to them!


    Marvel

    Avengers Movies

    If you want to get into marvel, I recommend watching the avengers movies. There are four movies in total and they are amazing! My favourite movie would probably be avengers end game or infinity war because they are the most exciting for me. I feel like you could watch these movies without seeing all of the other individual character movies because I watched the first avengers movie without having seen the other marvel movies.


    WandaVision

    This is one of my favourite TV shows to watch on Disney+. I mentioned this show in my January Favourites https://lifestyleseason.wordpress.com/2021/02/01/january-favorites/ so check that out to see my more detailed opinion on the show. The series has now finished (I'm sad it has finished) so you can watch all the episodes now! It is an incredible series because each episode is so exciting and I feel like it got even better as the series went on!


    Captain America Movies

    Honestly, it was so hard to pick one character's movies because I enjoy them all but I picked the Captain America movies because they are so interesting and I really like the backstory behind Captain America. My favourite would be Captain America Civil War because it is so action packed and I love that there are a lot of the marvel characters in this!


    Star

    Glee

    I've mentioned glee plenty of times on my blog but I had to put this in here! I love just watching my favourite episodes occasionally because it's a show that never gets old for me. The songs are incredible and I love the variety of pop, musical theatre, classics, etc. I will definitely be watching this a lot!


    The Other Woman

    I am so excited that this has come on Disney+. I haven't watched this movie in ages so I'll definitely have to watch it again soon! It is such an interesting movie and I love the characters. There are some comedy elements to this movie which I really like and I love watching it!


    The Maze Runner

    This is a brilliant film which I have watched many times! The storyline is so interesting and the character development is amazing. Unfortunately, I believe that only the first film is on Disney+ at the moment but hopefully the other films will come on it soon!
